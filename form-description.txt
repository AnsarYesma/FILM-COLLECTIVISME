FILM-COLLECTIVISME

"Only that which is lost is ever possessed." — Walter Benjamin

A collective filmmaking experiment where each participant contributes a 2-5 minute video interpreting this quote. The camera passes through trusted hands, creating a fragmented whole authored by no one and everyone.

RULES:
• Keep camera max 7 days
• Pass to someone you trust (no strangers)
• 12th person must return camera
• Don't delete/alter previous footage
• No filming without consent
• No hate speech/pornography
• Don't damage camera
• Fill this form for crediting
